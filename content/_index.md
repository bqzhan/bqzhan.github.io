# Brief Biography

Baoqiang Zhan is currently a second-year Ph.D. student of [School of Economics and Management](http://som.hit.edu.cn/en/), [Harbin Institute of Technology](http://en.hit.edu.cn/) (2022 QS rankings: **236**) in China.  He majors in management science and engineering. His reseach areas include *FinTech*, *Marketing-Finance Interface*, *Business Analytics*. He is interested in using different technologies (e.g., econometrics, machine learning and deep learning, etc.) to analyze the mechanisms behind the financial anomalies and the nature of investors behaviors.
